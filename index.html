MyMind

Your external brain for storing and retrieving ideas.

The Problem

In a world of constant information, we're bombarded with new ideas, articles, and tasks. Our brains can only hold so much, leading to a frustrating problem: **missing content when you need it most.**

That brilliant idea you had on your commute? The essential link you saved a month ago? They vanish, buried under a pile of files or forgotten in the chaos of your daily life. This isn't just an annoyance; it's a drain on our productivity and a source of unnecessary stress.

The Solution: MyMind

MyMind is a simple, intuitive application designed to be your external brain. It's a reliable, centralized place to store all your important information—from quick notes and voice memos to entire articles and images.

The real magic lies in its powerful search capability. MyMind doesn't just store data; it makes it instantly accessible. You can find anything you've ever saved in a matter of seconds by simply searching for a keyword.

Why MyMind?

Effortless

Designed with zero friction. Save content with a single click, and it intelligently organizes everything for you. No folders, no complex systems—just simple, smart organization.

Fast

Our advanced keyword-based search is incredibly powerful. No need to remember where you saved something or what you called it. Just type a related word, and MyMind will find it for you.

A Living Repository

MyMind helps you build a personal knowledge base that grows with you, making it easier to see patterns and connect ideas you might have forgotten.

How It Works

1. Capture

When you find something you want to save—a note, a link, or an image—you simply save it to MyMind.

2. Organize

MyMind's engine automatically tags and indexes your content, suggesting relevant keywords based on the information you've saved.

3. Retrieve

When you need to find something, just open the app and type a keyword or a phrase into the search bar. MyMind will instantly pull up all related content.
